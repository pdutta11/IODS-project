Chapter 2: Regression Analysis

About the data

In this exercise we are going to analyze student’s attitude towards statistics, based on the survey conducted in 2014. The initial survey was conducted in Finnish and was measured on the Likert Scale(1-5).

For the analysis, we convert the original data to a tabular format where each column represents questions and rows contain individual response from the participants. The items of the central measure are named so that the connections to the corresponding dimensions (Deep/SUrface/STrategic) can be easily seen. The analysis will be carried across 7 variables: “age”, “gender”, “attitude”, “deep”, “strategic”, “surface” and “points” distributed in 7 columns and on 166 student observations.

More information on data can be found [here][https://www.mv.helsinki.fi/home/kvehkala/JYTmooc/JYTOPKYS3-meta.txt].
